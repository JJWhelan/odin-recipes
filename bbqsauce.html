Mustard BBQ Sauce

Description

This is a great version of the traditional mustard based bbq sauce. Most sauces are ketchup based, but the Carolinas they prefer the mustard variation. You should try it at least once.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup prepared yellow mustard
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, stir together the mustard, honey, brown sugar and vinegar.
  2. Season with black pepper.
  3. Bring to a boil, and let simmer for 5 minutes.
